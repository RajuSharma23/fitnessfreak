Description

Cappuccino is a classic Italian coffee drink, made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and a layer of frothy milk foam on top. Known for its rich and balanced flavor, it offers a creamy texture that enhances the bold espresso taste. Often enjoyed in the morning, cappuccino can be customized with a sprinkle of cinnamon or cocoa powder for an extra touch of flavor. At The Coffee Nook, we craft this popular drink to perfection, providing coffee lovers with a smooth, velvety experience that blends milk and espresso in perfect harmony.
